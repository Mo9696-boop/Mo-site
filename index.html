Who I Am

I did my PhD in oncology. Cancer research. The kind of field where the distance between a discovery and a patient who needs it can feel unbridgeable. That gap convinced me early that the most capable science is not the science that gets published - it's the science that leaves the lab.

Founder - CVLab
2025 - Present
Building a mentoring platform, two newsletters, and a community of scientists brave enough to think beyond the lab.
PhD in Oncology - CRCM, Aix-Marseille University
2021 - 2024
Four years studying how telomeres behave under stress, developing chromatin-mapping assays from scratch, and learning that rigorous science is only half the work.
Master's Researcher - AUB & ERIBA
2019
Two labs, two countries, one project on senolytic compounds. Learned early that good science requires as much coordination as it does pipetting.
